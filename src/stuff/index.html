Music

I fell in love with synthesizers and their way of making sound, such as DX's by Yamaha, Prophets by Sequential, Jupiters and Junos by Roland. I started experimenting with synthesis and creating sound patches, basically math that sounds good.

Physical synthesizers can cost thousands of dollars, but virtual synthesizers can emulate their sounds effectively. To share the fun I had, I built a simple web synthesizer to let you explore how they work.

For professional production, I recommend Vital — it's free and has great visual feedback and intuitive controls for both beginners and experts. You can find my sound library here.

I also own a Roland DJ-808 and occasionally DJ for guests and friends.
