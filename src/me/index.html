I'm a Gearhead

I enjoy working on cars and love to bring my 2008 Nissan 350Z on race tracks. Its naturally-aspirated 3.5L VQ35HR produces around 320 hp and 275 lb-ft of torque with minor bolt-on mods. The car currently runs on a set of 18'' BBS RG rims with Bridgestone Potenza's in 265/35 all-around.

My current goal is to build a time-attack & show street car, but might swing my mind and do a drift build somewhere down the line.

Feel free to direct message me if you want to go on a track day with me around Toronto!
