Synths

I have learned to produce, mix, and master music. I fell in love with synthesizers — DX-7, Prophet-5, Jupiter-8, Moog Model D, and Juno-60.

Sound Design

A well-maintained physical synthesizer can cost thousands of dollars! Fortunately, there are a number of virtual synthesizers that can emulate their iconic sounds. A strong recommendation from me is Vital. It is an amazing piece for both beginers and experts with great visual feedback, intuitive LFO and envelope controls, and most importantly it is free!
