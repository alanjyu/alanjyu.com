Synethsizers

Mathematical waves can sound beautiful and form the foundation of electronic music. Classic synthesizers like DX's, Prophet's, and Jupiter's, and Juno's. I enjoy building sound patches from scratch and shaping tones that feel alive.

Instead of spending thousands for an antique, I recommend starting with software VST's, such as Vital. It is free-and-open-source, sounds clean, and its interface is intuitive for both beginners and experts alike. You can check out some of my sound patches here.
